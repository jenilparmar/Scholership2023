Gujarat Bhartiya Yuva Vikas Yojana also known as BYVS, BYVS Scholarship is a scholarship scheme that is offered to the economically weaker section. The scholarship is open to students who are enrolled in undergraduate and postgraduate courses in recognized universities and colleges in Gujarat. The scholarship amount is Rs. 5,000 per year for students pursuing class 11 and 12, Rs. 10,000 per year for students pursuing graduation, and Rs. 15,000 per year for students pursuing post-graduation. Bhartiya Yuva Vikas Schoolership(BYVS) is a government-funded scholarship program in Gujarat that provides financial assistance to students from economically weaker sections who are pursuing higher education. The scholarship is administered by the Education Department of the Government of Gujarat, and it is open to students who are domiciled in Gujarat and who meet the eligibility criteria.
